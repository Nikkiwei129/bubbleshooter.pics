About Bubble Shooter

How to Play

Bubble Shooter is a classic puzzle game where your goal is to clear the screen by shooting and popping colorful bubbles. Match three or more bubbles of the same color to make them pop and score points!

Simple Controls

Aim with your mouse or touchscreen and click to shoot bubbles. Plan your shots carefully to create chain reactions!

Score Points

Clear bubbles in large groups to earn bonus points. Challenge yourself to beat your high score!

Bubble Shooter Strategy Tips

Plan Your Shots

Look for opportunities to create chain reactions rather than just shooting at the obvious match.

Aim for the Edges

Try to pop bubbles on the edges to create cascading effects and clear large sections at once.

Use Special Bubbles

Take advantage of special bubbles when they appear to clear difficult sections of the board.

Think Strategically

Consider your next few moves, not just the current shot, to set up bigger combos later.
